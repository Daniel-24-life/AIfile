# Concept Card: QA Preview Before Delivery

## Insight

Generated documents and workbooks need visual review before delivery.

## Meaning

Structured files can exist and still be wrong. Images may crop poorly, tables may overflow, fields may be missing, and layout can break after export.

## Rule

Before release, create preview artifacts:

- contact sheets
- rendered document previews
- spreadsheet screenshots
- checklist pages
- missing-field summaries

## Reuse

Use this card for portfolio packages, submission materials, reports, slide decks, and AI-generated document sets.
